Will Ferrell, Kevin Hart, and Jimmy Fallon Compete in the Super Bowl of Lip Sync Battles

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 18, 2024

In honor of last night’s Super Bowl, Jimmy Fallon staged his most high-octane lip-sync battle yet, featuring the Get Hard stars Will Ferrell and Kevin Hart. It’s a wild 13 minutes with endless highlights: Will Ferrell becomes Will Fierce for Beyoncé’s “Drunk in Love,” the mike stand has to be adjusted a couple of times, and Fallon rips holes in his suit with a surprise guest and scary dance moves. Set it on repeat and settle in, because it’s one of those things you can watch over and over and find something new each time.
