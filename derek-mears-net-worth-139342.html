About

American actor who became known for his roles in horror films, including the remake of Friday the 13th in 2009 and the 2013 movie Hatchet III. In 2005, he portrayed Lead Zorgon in the film Zathura. He has also done Stunts for several installments of the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise.
