Full Deck achievement in Cult of the Lamb

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 4, 2024
After Relics of the Old Faith, there are some extra cards you'll need to collect. Thanks to VoltixX1 for their locations:
  • The Intangible - Purchase in a Monch room (meeting him is one of possibilities when entering random room, luck based)
  • Retribution - Purchase from the shop in Pilgrim's Passage
  • Wraith's Will - Purchase from the shop in Smuggler's Sanctuary
  • Godly Moment - Purchase from the shop in Midas' Cave
  • Kin of Turua - Purchase from the shop in Spore Grotto
  • The Collector - Purchase from Chemach's shop (while on crusade)
  • Consecrated Oil - Complete Ratoo's challenge for the first time
Here are all the original cards and where to locate them:
  • The Hearts I - part of the deck you start the game with
  • The Hearts II - Beat Ratau at Knucklebones
  • The Hearts III - Purchase from Ratoo, found on a heart floor in a dungeon, for 100 coins
  • Weeping Moon - purchase in Spore Grotto for 60 coins
  • Nature's Boon - purchase in Pilgrim's Passage for 25 coins
  • The Lovers I - part of the deck you start the game with
  • The Lovers II - receive after marrying your first follower or running your first fighting pit (requires the Wedding Ritual or Fighting Pit Ritual under the Law & Order doctrine, thanks Geck Brev)
  • All Seeing Sun - purchase in Pilgrim's Passage for 25 coins
  • True Sight - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Telescope - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Hands of Rage - purchase in Pilgrim's Passage for 25 coins
  • The Burning Dead - purchase in Midas's Cave for 10 gold bars
  • Diseased Heart - part of the deck you start the game with
  • The Arachnid - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Divine Strength - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Master of the Art - part of the deck you start the game with
  • The Path - purchase in Spore Grotto for 100 coins
  • Fervour's Harvest - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Soul Snatcher - purchase in Smuggler's Sanctuary for 150 coins
  • Shield of Faith - part of the deck you start the game with
  • The Bomb - purchase in Smuggler's Sanctuary for 100 coins
  • Ichor Earned - purchase in Midas's Cave for 10 gold bars
  • Ichor Lingered - purchase in Smuggler's Sanctuary for 125 coins
  • Mithridatism - Beat Flinky at Knucklebones
  • Blazing Trail - purchase in Spore Grotto for 100 coins
  • Divine Curse - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Strength from Within - Beat Shrumy at Knucklebones
  • Strength from Without - Beat Klunko & Bop at Knucklebones
  • Neptunes Curse - Receive from The Fisherman after catching your first fish
  • Fortune's Blessing - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Death's Door - part of the deck you start the game with
  • The Deal - purchase in Midas's Cave for 20 gold bars
  • Rabbits Foot - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Ambrosia - purchase from Forneus, found on a vendor floor in a dungeon, for 30 coins
  • Gift From Below - part of the deck you start the game with
  • Fervour's Host - Receive from Helob, found on a vendor floor in a dungeon, after purchasing 'enough' followers from him (random amount)
Please let me know if you have any issues or questions!
