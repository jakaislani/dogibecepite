Released WWE Stars Emerge with Exciting New Tag Team Name

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Mace and Mansoor, the two members of Maximum Male Models’ stable, had a lot of potential in the company. Fans wanted to see them booked properly, but that never happened. They would end up getting released and now their new tag team name has been unveiled.

During the company’s latest slew of releases back in September, Mace and Mansoor were handed their release. This came as a shock for many fans who wanted to see them succeed in WWE.

It was officially announced recently that Mace and Mansoor will be appearing for their first post-WWE pro wrestling event, as they will be making their GCW debut at Effy’s Big Gay Brunch on January 27, 2024. They are now simply known as MxM – somewhat minimalistic but effective nonetheless.

Both Mace and Mansoor have 90-day non-compete clauses and will be free agents from December 20th. Only time will tell whether they will eventually make their way back to WWE, as the company may find value in them in the future.
