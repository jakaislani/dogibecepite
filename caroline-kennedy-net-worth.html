Caroline Kennedy | Politics, Books & Investments

Politics

Caroline belongs to the democratic party. She was Ambassador of the United States in Japan from 2013-2017.

In June 2008, Barack Obama appointed her to co-chair his Vice Presidential Search Committee, alongside Jim Johnson and Eric Holder.

Additionally, in May 2021, Joe Biden was considering her to be the Ambassador of the United States in Australia.

She is also a member of the bar associations of New York and Washington, D.C.

Books

Kennedy and Ellen Alderman have written two books together on civil liberties; In Our Defense: The Bill of Rights In Action in 1991 and The Right to Privacy in 1995.

Kennedy has edited The Best Loved Poems of Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis (2002) and A Patriot’s Handbook (2003), A Family of Poems: My Favorite Poetry for Children (2005).

In 2011, Caroline edited and introduced a new poetry collection, She Walks In Beauty.

She launched the book at the John F Kennedy Library & Museum of Columbia Point, Dorchester, MA.
