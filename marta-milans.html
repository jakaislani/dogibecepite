Marta Milans is a Spanish Amerian actress who has worked in American, Spanish, and British film industries. She is known for her work in the films, Devoured (2012), The Disappearance of Eleanor Rigby (2014), Asher (2018), and Shazam! (2019).

Wiki/Biography

Marta Milans was born on Monday, 19 April 1982 (age 38 years; as in 2020) in Madrid, Spain. Her zodiac sign is Aries.

Marta Milans as a Child

She grew up in Ibiza, Spain. When she was a teen, she moved to New York City. She did a double major in Art History and Acting from New York University.

Family & Ethnicity

Her Father’s name is Alfonso Pérez-Andújar. Her mother’s name is Marta Cavestany. Her brother’s name is Sander. Both her father and brother are the owner of their organic cheese farm, Santa Gadea in Spain. Her sister, Gadea Pérez-Andújar is a jeweller.

Career

In Films

She made her debut with the American film, Love in the Age of Dion (2006) as ‘Carol.’ She has also acted in short films, the first one being Luchador Love (2007) as ‘Nina.’ She has acted in a few shorts; Scandal (2009) and The Portrait (2011) being two of them. She made her British film debut with Shame (2011) as ‘Cocktail Waitress.’

She landed a lead role in the award-winning American film, Devoured (2012) as ‘Lourdes.’ The film was premiered at the 2012 Bilbao Fantasy Film Festival. Later, she starred in the American films, The Disappearance of Eleanor Rigby (2014), Asher (2018), and Shazam! (2019).

In Television

She made her Spanish debut with the TV series, La piel azul (2010) as ‘Carlota.’

The same year, she acted in the Spanish TV series, Valientes as ‘Alba Varela.’ In 2011, she made her American TV debut with the mega-hit series, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit (2011) as ‘Imelda Barros.’

In 2014, she landed a major role in the American TV series, Killer Women (2014) as ‘Becca Parker.’

She has starred in a few American series, such as The Death of Eva Sofia Valdez (2016), The Mysteries of Laura (2016), High Maintenance (2016), and No Tomorrow (2016-2017). She has also starred in a few popular Spanish series, such as Vergüenza (2017), El embarcadero (2019), and White Lines (2020).
